About Us

The Premier Volleyball League is the Philippines' first-ever women's professional volleyball league organized by Sports Vision Management Group, Inc.

The league began in 2004 as the Shakey's V-League, a women's collegiate league. The Shakey's V-League changed its name to the Premier Volleyball League (PVL) starting the 2017 season.

Since 2011, the league became open to participation of corporate clubs and other non-collegiate teams.In November 2020,

the PVL announced that the league would turn professional starting with the 2021 season. PVL will have its first ever pro Open Conference in July 2021,

in a bubble format in Ilocos NortePVL games will live streamed on pvl.ph in the Philippines and internationally.

They will also be available on Cignal TV.
